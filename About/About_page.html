Our Story

Born on the road,
built in the garage

DMototech started the way every good riding story does — with a problem on the road. Our founder, a passionate tourer, couldn't find touring accessories that actually worked for Indian conditions. So he built his own.

What began as DIY modifications in a small garage soon turned into a full-fledged manufacturing operation. Today, under the name Dochaki, we design and manufacture rider-focused touring accessories that are tested on real Indian highways, through real weather, by real riders.

We're not a big corporation. We're a small team that rides, understands pain points firsthand, and builds solutions that actually last.
